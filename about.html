About Project

SmartParkAI — Vision Intelligence for Smart Cities

SmartParkAI is a vision-based smart parking system that transforms raw CCTV video into actionable analytics for operators and city planners. By combining a TensorFlow CNN model with OpenCV-based region-of-interest (ROI) extraction, the system can detect individual parking slots and classify them as parked or empty in near real-time.

The backend is built on Flask and orchestrates video ingestion, ROI sampling, model inference, parking state tracking, and billing computation. The CNN model is trained to understand the visual patterns of occupied versus free bays, while OpenCV handles low-level video processing and grid-based slot segmentation.

On top of this, the dashboard exposes occupancy trends, revenue curves, and granular parking events to give a control-center view of the parking lot. This makes SmartParkAI suitable for smart campuses, malls, transit hubs, and city-scale parking orchestration.
